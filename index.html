Introduction

HIGH-TECH Engineering Solutions is Kenya's leading and fast growing steel fabrication unit, providing engineering services to a host of competent places across the globe.

Incorporated in 2016, HIGH-TECH ENGINEERING SOLUTIONS is a private Limited Company. It was acquired by the Director Mr. Oscar Obul. Then the company had only 3 employees and since then Mr. Obul has worked to give it a new look of life and turn it into Kenya's leading steel fabrication company.

Anything in steel is coined by him and he has been the guiding principle since 2016 through his excellent and innovative ideas.

The company's core competency lies in heavy steel fabrication, supply of welding machines and assembly of steel structures, materials handling, equipment and steel processing for domestic and international projects.

HIGH-TECH is competing for the worlds No.1 position in Fabrication by providing quality services to all our clients.

Presently the company has been investing heavily in highly modern Security steel doors, Windows, Gates, Balcony and staircase grills, Godown steel structures, Steel water towers, and All types of steel works.

Having a workforce of over 30 employees. Major expansions are underway with plans to have all the required machines instilled for streamlining production around the world.
